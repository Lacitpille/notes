## Inline Math

## Display Math

The Gaussian integral:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \sqrt{\pi}$$

Fourier transform:

$$\hat{f}(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)\, e^{-2\pi i x \xi} \, dx$$

Stokes' theorem:

$$\oint_{\partial \Omega} \omega = \int_{\Omega} d\omega$$

## Matrix
